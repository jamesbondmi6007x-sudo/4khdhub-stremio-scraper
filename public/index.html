4KHDHub Scraper

A Stremio addon that scrapes 4khdhub.dad for high-quality movies and series links.

Install to Stremio

Features:

  • 🎬 Fetches 1080p, 4K, HDR, and DV links
  • 🔗 Provides direct external URLs to HubCloud/HubDrive
  • ⚡ Fast and lightweight
  • 🛡️ MediaFlow Proxy support to bypass Cloudflare
